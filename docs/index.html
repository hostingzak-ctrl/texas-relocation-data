Dataset: Texas No-Income-Tax Break-Even (2026)

Texas has no state income tax but high property taxes. For a married-filing-jointly household with $200,000 taxable income, this dataset computes the home value at which Texas property tax erases the income tax saved by leaving the origin state.

Origin stateIncome tax savedBreak-even (TX @ 1.80%)
Illinois$9,900Texas always cheaper
New York$10,864~$2.17M home
California$11,477~$1.04M home
Florida$0Texas always costs more
Washington$0Texas always costs more

Florida and Washington movers pay more in Texas at any home price — no income tax to save plus lower property taxes at home. The "no-income-tax mirage."
